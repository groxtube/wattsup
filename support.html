üîå How do I add my appliances to WattsUp?

üìä How accurate are the kWh calculations?

WattsUp uses industry-standard calculations based on appliance wattage and usage time. For the most accurate results, ensure you enter precise wattage ratings and realistic usage hours.

üì± How do I manually enter meter readings?

Go to the manual reading entry screen and input your current meter reading along with the date. WattsUp will calculate the difference and track your consumption over time.

Yes! Set your electricity rate in the settings and WattsUp will estimate your monthly bills based on your tracked consumption. Note: Rates vary by location and provider.

üîß Troubleshooting Tips

  • Inaccurate Consumption Calculations Double-check your appliance wattage ratings and usage hours. Ensure all appliances are properly categorized and rated.

  • Bill Estimates Not Matching Verify your electricity rate is set correctly in settings. Rates vary by region and provider - check your latest electricity bill.

  • Data Not Saving Check that you have sufficient storage space on your device. Data is stored locally and requires available device storage.
