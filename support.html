Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I enable location services?

To get accurate weather forecasts, ensure location services are enabled for WattsUp in your device settings. Go to Settings > Privacy > Location Services > WattsUp and select "While Using the App".

Why isn't my location detected accurately?

Make sure you have a stable internet connection and GPS enabled. If you're indoors, try going outside or near a window for better GPS reception.

How do I change the temperature units?

In the app settings, you can toggle between Celsius and Fahrenheit. Look for the settings icon (gear) in the main menu.

I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?

Check your device notification settings for WattsUp. Ensure notifications are enabled and the app has permission to send alerts. You can manage notification preferences within the app.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We value your feedback! Use the contact information below to reach out with bug reports, feature requests, or general suggestions.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Your location data is used only for weather services and is not stored permanently. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • App Crashing: Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If issues persist, restart your device.
  • Slow Performance: Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Clear app cache if available in settings.
  • Outdated Information: Pull down to refresh weather data or check your internet connection.
