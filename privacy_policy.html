WattsUp is your universal energy consumption tracking companion. We are committed to protecting your privacy while helping you monitor and optimize your energy usage worldwide.

1. Introduction

WattsUp ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App") for universal kWh energy consumption tracking.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

Energy Consumption Data

The App allows you to track your energy consumption through:

  • Manual meter reading entries
  • Appliance specifications and usage patterns
  • Historical consumption data
  • Location information for regional energy insights

Device Information

We may collect information about your device, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Network connectivity information

Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the App, such as:

  • Features accessed and time spent
  • Appliances added and managed
  • Consumption readings entered
  • Analytics and insights viewed
  • Crash reports and performance data

Location Data (Optional)

With your permission, we may collect:

  • Approximate location for regional energy statistics
  • Weather information for energy optimization tips

Advertising Data

For ad personalization, our advertising partners may collect:

  • Device advertising ID
  • Ad interaction data
  • Usage patterns for relevant advertising

We use the collected information to:

Your energy consumption data is primarily stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest

  • Access controls and authentication measures

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:
